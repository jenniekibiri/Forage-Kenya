Complete Kenyan Growing Guide

Tailored for Kenya's unique climate and soil conditions

üå± Land Preparation

STEP 1
  • Deep plow to 30cm during Kenya's dry season
  • Harrow and level for fine tilth
  • Test soil pH (ideal: 5.0-8.0 for Kenyan soils)
  • Add agricultural lime if pH below 5.0
  • Apply 10 tons/acre farmyard manure

üåø Tumbukiza Planting Method

STEP 2
  • Make furrows 1.2 meters apart
  • Use healthy 3-4 month cuttings with 3 nodes
  • Plant at 70cm spacing within rows
  • Bury 2 nodes, leave 1 above ground
  • Plant at start of Kenya's rainy season

üíß Maintenance Schedule

ONGOING
  • Weekly watering during dry season
  • NPK 17:17:17 after each harvest (50kg/acre)
  • Weed control for first 3 months
  • Monitor for Napier stunt disease
  • Mulch with dry grass during hot seasons
